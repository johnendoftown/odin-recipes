Recipe: Sushi

Description

Sushi is a classic Japanese dish with origins best described, in good comedic fashion, here. Sushi is a notable cultural import form Japan, consisting of sticky rice stuck to a dried sheet of seaweed, rolled with seafood and vegetables to fill the roll. Making sushi is daunting at first, and often takes many tries to master and come out with a clean looking roll. This guide will cover the basics or rolling your own roll, using a califonia roll as an example. Sushi is very flexible and can be added in, on, or to with a variety of different ingredients and techniques! I'll cover alternate options and techniques as we go, so let's begin!
